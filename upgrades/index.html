Survival Games - Platinum VIP - Platinum VIP players receive full iron armour and a diamond sword. They can get more advantages than any other Survival Games VIP rank.

Survival Games - Gold VIP - Gold VIP players receive full chain armour and a gold sword. They have an advantage above most of the other Survival Games VIP ranks.

Survival Games - Silver VIP - Silver VIP players receive full gold armour and an iron sword. They have an advantage above the free Bronze VIP rank.

Survival Games - Bronze VIP(free) - Bronze VIP players receive full leather armour and a wooden sword. This rank is free to all players to equip and receive.
